# Why Straight Through Processing Is Important for Insurers

![](/images/blog/66a8904cf45034a063d9f07b_Through-Processing-Is-Important-for-Insurers_1.jpeg)

Why Straight Through Processing Is Important for Insurers

Straight through processing (STP) is a game-changer for insurers, automating tasks and streamlining workflows. This translates to faster claims processing, fewer errors, and happier customers – a win-win for both insurers and their bottom line.

In the ever-growing insurance industry, where post-pandemic demands are high, straight through processing (STP insurance) is emerging as a critical tool. [A 2021 Deloitte survey](<https://www2.deloitte.com/us/en/insights/industry/financial-services/impact-of-pandemic-insurance-industry.html?distinct_id=018d7d46-4615-7f74-ab32-f03be14737ce>) underscores this point, emphasizing the growing need for technological advancements to improve both efficiency and customer experience. STP automates tasks like claims payments and underwriting, offering significant advantages for insurers aiming to maintain client satisfaction and navigate industry growth. This blog delves deeper into STP, exploring its impact on insurer workflows and revenue, and highlighting the role of intelligent document processing in facilitating a smooth STP implementation.

## What is Straight Through Processing (STP) in Insurance?

![](/images/blog/66a890867d4a678b00d7e115_Through-Processing-Is-Important-for-Insurers_3.jpeg)

What is Straight Through Processing (STP) in Insurance?

Straight through processing, as defined by Investopedia, is an “automated process done purely through electronic transfers with no manual intervention involved.” Essentially, documents are handled in a single, streamlined process within a computing system, eliminating the need for them to bounce between departments or require additional human input.

**STP in Action: Automating Insurance Processes**   
Technological advancements like AI and machine learning are making it possible for insurers to leverage STP for tasks like:

  * **Underwriting:** Streamlining the application process by automatically analyzing data to determine risk and eligibility.
  * **Claims Processing:** Automating claim payments and initial assessments, particularly for simpler personal and P&C claims. [A 2021 McKinsey report](<https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/financial-services/our-insights/how-data-and-analytics-are-redefining-excellence-in-p-and-c-underwriting?distinct_id=018d7d46-4615-7f74-ab32-f03be14737ce>) suggests P&C insurance could see a doubling of STP rates, driven by reductions in manual data entry of up to 90%.



While full STP for complex claim applications is still evolving, insurers are rapidly adopting it for faster claim payments. Moving away from paper checks and manual processing, STP allows for efficient digital payments, benefiting both policyholders with quicker settlements and insurers with faster administrative processes.

The advantages of straight through processing for insurance are significant, making it no wonder it's at the forefront of technology adoption within the industry.

## The Allure of Straight Through Processing (STP) for Insurers

![](/images/blog/66a890b3c58fcde9552202b6_Through-Processing-Is-Important-for-Insurers_4.jpeg)

The Allure of Straight Through Processing (STP) for Insurers

In the ever-competitive insurance landscape, efficiency and customer satisfaction are paramount. Straight through processing (STP) insurance emerges as a game-changer, offering a compelling solution for insurers seeking to improve on both fronts. This blog explores the key benefits of STP and how it addresses the needs of both insurers and clients.

**What are the benefits of straight-through processing insurance?**   
Modern consumers expect speed and convenience, and insurance claims are no exception. Lengthy claim processing times can lead to frustration and customer churn. Here's how STP tackles this challenge:

  * **Faster Processing:** Automation significantly reduces claim and underwriting processing times.
  * **Improved Service for Simple Claims:** For straightforward P&C claims, STP expedites document processing, leading to quicker resolutions and happier customers.**‍**
  * **Focus on Complex Cases:** By freeing up employees from routine tasks, STP allows them to dedicate more time to resolving intricate claims, benefiting all policyholders.



**Optimizing Resources and Boosting Efficiency**   
STP offers a win-win for both insurers and their employees. Here's how it streamlines operations and reduces costs:

  * **Reduced Errors:** Automation minimizes human error, leading to greater accuracy and efficiency.
  * **Improved Workflow:** Automating document and claim processing frees up employees to focus on higher-value tasks.
  * **Lower Operational Costs:** Fewer errors and a streamlined workflow translate to significant cost savings for insurers.
  * **Strategic Resource Allocation:** Skilled employees can dedicate their expertise to complex cases, maximizing their impact.



**The Bottom Line: A Financially Sound Choice**   
STP offers a clear path to financial benefits for insurers, ultimately leading to a healthier bottom line:

  * **Increased Revenue:** Faster claim processing translates to happier customers and higher retention rates.
  * **Reduced Costs:** Automation minimizes errors and streamlines workflows, leading to significant cost savings.



By leveraging STP, insurers can optimize their operations, enhance customer experience, and ultimately achieve greater financial success.

## The Foundation of STP: Accessible and Usable Data

![](/images/blog/66a890728860ed4b63f1a149_Through-Processing-Is-Important-for-Insurers_2.jpeg)

The Foundation of STP: Accessible and Usable Data

Straight through processing (STP) relies heavily on the accessibility and quality of data. Without it, automation becomes a fantasy. This is why the first crucial step in implementing STP involves automating data extraction from documents.

**Enter**[**Intelligent Document Processing**](<../products/idp.html>)**(IDP)**. This technology eliminates the need for manual data entry, regardless of the document format or source. Imagine this: a document is scanned, and like magic, all the relevant information is automatically extracted and integrated into the system, ready for use. This not only happens significantly faster than manual entry, but also with far less risk of errors.

Consider a car accident claim with a submitted inspection report. IDP would swiftly extract all the pertinent data from the report, converting it into a structured, usable format. An STP system powered by AI could then automatically assess the claim based on this extracted data. This exemplifies the essence of STP – documents enter the system and are processed directly, eliminating the need for human intervention at multiple stages. In this instance, there's no human interaction at all.

However, the foundation of this streamlined process lies in automated data extraction. A single claim often involves a multitude of documents, both physical and digital. IDP tackles this challenge seamlessly, whether the files include receipts, medical reports, or other documents. It transforms this information into a structured format, which is the fuel that powers the rest of the efficient STP system.
